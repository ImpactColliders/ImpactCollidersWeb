About Impact Colliders

Impact Colliders creates games to solve problems in society. Our game, "Eh How Ah?", is a family-friendly card game that helps seniors learn how to use technology and protect themselves against scams in a fun and approachable manner while fostering intergenerational bonding.

Our Approach

We tap on both public and private support to keep "Eh How Ah?" sustainable for the long run, so that more can enjoy the game to bridge the gap in digital literacy and fight against scammers. Thanks to government support, we're able to conduct digital literacy workshops for active aging centers at no cost. We're always innovating, so funds from customers and corporates go toward product development and improvement. If you have a CSR project, we offer turnkey solutions—drop us an enquiry.
